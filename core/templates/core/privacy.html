Privacy Policy

At Fusion AI, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We understand the sensitive nature of resumes, job descriptions, and personal information, and we are committed to protecting your data with the utmost care.

1. Data Collection

When you use our platform, you may be asked to upload your resume and job description. This data is used solely to generate personalized resume matching scores, interview questions, and career insights.

2. Temporary Data Storage

We do not store your resume or job description permanently. Uploaded files are processed in-memory or stored temporarily in a secure session and are automatically deleted once the session ends or after a short period of inactivity.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never shared with any third parties. Fusion AI does not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information or uploaded content to advertisers or any external organizations.

4. Session-Based Analysis

Resume analysis and interview preparation are done within your logged-in session. After logging out, your uploaded content is discarded from the system.

5. Security Measures

We employ best practices to safeguard your data, including secure HTTPS encryption, access restrictions, and automated cleanup of temporary data.

6. Your Control

You are in full control of your data. You can choose to avoid uploading any files and instead paste text manually.

7. Responsible AI Use

Fusion AI is powered by AI models. While we aim to provide helpful and relevant suggestions, we advise users to review all generated content carefully before using it for professional purposes.
